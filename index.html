This website is intended to serve as a guide for individuals seeking information on piracy. As a proponent of piracy, I am utilizing my platform to educate others. I hold the view that if purchasing does not equate to ownership, then piracy should not be equated with theft. Presented herein is a curated list of reliable websites and software for pirating.

VPNs

  • Mullvad - Considered one of the best VPNs available. It doesn't support port forwarding but is highly secure and privacy-focused. Based in Sweden. (¹)
  • ProtonVPN - Supports port forwarding and is known for its strong privacy policies. Based in Switzerland.
  • CloudFlare Warp - A virtually free VPN that doesn't support port forwarding. Best used with Wireguard. (¹) Use with a Warp+ key for enhanced performance.

Torrent Clients

My top recommendation is qBittorrent (open-source). It's widely regarded as the standard. Avoid using µTorrent or BitTorrent as they are known to include adware, cryptominers, and spyware. ¹

Other reputable open-source alternatives include Transmission and Deluge.

Browser Extensions

  • uBlock Origin - The best ad and content blocker for both Chromium and Firefox.
  • FastForward - Bypass link shorteners effortlessly.

Games

  • FitGirl Repacks (DDL / Torrent) - Renowned for highly compressed game repacks. Offers Torrent links with DDL mirrors. Installation time varies based on your hardware.
  • DODI Repacks (DDL / Torrent) - Another reputable repacker providing Torrent links with DDL mirrors. Generally faster installation times compared to FitGirl, but with slightly larger file sizes.
  • GOG Games, .onion (DDL) - DRM-free games from GOG, re-uploaded with original installers and SHA-256 hashes for verification.
  • GamesDrive (DDL) - Hosts repacks, P2P cracks, and scene releases. Also accepts game requests.
  • Online-Fix (DDL / Torrent) - Provides multiplayer fixes for various games, along with Torrent links.
  • CS.RIN.RU, .onion (DDL) - A top forum for game piracy. Account required.

ROMs

  • Vimm's Lair: Vault (DDL / Torrent) - Offers a variety of ROMs with slow download speeds. Torrents available for larger files like Wii games.
  • nsw2u (DDL) - Provides NSP and XCI files for Switch games, including updates.
  • NoPayStation (DDL) - The best source for PSP, PS3, and PS Vita games, directly from Sony's CDN with decryption keys.
