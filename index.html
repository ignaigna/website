This website is intended to serve as a guide for individuals seeking information on piracy. As a proponent of piracy, I am utilizing my platform to educate others. I hold the view that if purchasing does not equate to ownership, then piracy should not be equated with theft. Presented herein is a curated list of reliable websites and software for pirating.

VPNs

  • Mullvad - Arguably the best VPN available (¹), but doesn't support port forwarding. Based in Sweden.
  • ProtonVPN - Supports port forwarding, based in Switzerland.

Torrent Clients

Only recommendation I have is qBittorrent (open-source). It's essentially the standard now. Do NOT use µTorrent or BitTorrent. They're known to ship with adware, cryptominers, and spyware. ¹

Browser Extensions

  • uBlock Origin - The best Ad & Content blocker for Chromium and Firefox.
  • FastForward - Circumvent link shorteners.

Software Sites

  • m0nkrus - Trusted Adobe software site. Windows only.
  • MAS (Microsoft Activation Scripts) - Your all-in-one solution to activating Windows & Microsoft Office.

Games

  • FitGirl Repacks (DDL / Torrent) - One of the most well known and reputable repackers out there. They provide Torrent links with DDL mirrors. Install time can be dependent on the hardware in your computer.
  • DODI Repacks (DDL / Torrent) - Another active repacker that provides Torrent links with DDL mirrors. May take less time to install compared to FitGirl, coming at a cost with slightly larger releases.
  • GOG Games (DDL) - GOG DRM-free Games, ripped and re-uploaded on Mirrors. Every installer is untouched and has SHA-256 hashes from GOG.
  • GamesDrive (DDL) - Re-uploads Repacks, P2P Cracks and Scene Releases to various hosts. Also take requests.
  • Online-Fix (DDL / Torrent) - Provides "Online-Fixes" (Multiplayer) for many Games available; coded by their own Crackers. Also provides Torrents.

ROMs

  • Vimm's Lair: Vault (DDL / Torrent) - Download speed's a bit slow. Torrents are provided for larger downloads; see Wii Games.
  • nsw2u (DDL) - NSP / XCI files for Switch Games; Updates included.
